Aplikasi Kedai Pasta

  • Category: Projects (Study Case)

  • Description: Aplikasi Kedai Pasta that offers original recipes and high-quality ingredients, catering to customers across Indonesia. Our primary challenge revolved around the users' difficulty in finding their favorite pasta dishes amidst a plethora of options available in the app. The complex layout and navigation frustrated users, as they lacked clear information about each pasta menu, including ingredients, flavors, and available variations. With the goal of providing the best possible solution for our users, our team focused on delivering an intuitive and user-friendly navigation system. We wanted to ensure that users could swiftly and effortlessly discover their desired pasta dishes. To achieve this, we endeavored to implement a menu recommendation feature based on user preferences and order history. By adopting a personalized approach, we aimed to provide a unique and tailored experience for each user. The end result was an alluring and straightforward pasta ordering app. Users can now easily navigate to their favorite pasta menus, access clear information about each dish, and explore a diverse range of flavors. I am immensely proud of the team's contributions in delivering a solution that has had a positive impact on our users. This project taught me the importance of attentively listening to user needs and providing relevant and satisfying experiences. We hope that this pasta ordering app will become a loyal companion for pasta enthusiasts throughout Indonesia. Thank you for taking the time to follow my design journey, and please feel free to provide feedback or ask any questions you may have.
